@def title = "About my name"

There are multiple other people named "Jack Garzella",
i.e. that have the exact same first and last name as me.
Thus, for as long as I can remember everyone has called
me "JJ", which comes from my first and middle initials.

### How to refer to me

If you're referring to me in a formal context (for example, a citation),
please use my full name with middle initial, 
i.e. "Jack J. Garzella" or "Jack J Garzella".
If the context is informal (for example, talking in person),
feel free to call me JJ!
