I often teach [Calculus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Calculus).

If you're wondering why you would want to learn Calculus,\\
take a look at [some things calculus is used for](/assets/calculus_used_for.pdf).

If you're looking for a calculus textbook, check out
the [Saxon Math Calculus](https://www.amazon.com/Calculus-Trigonometry-Analytic-Geometry-Saxon/dp/156577146X)
book.\\
This was the book that I learned from, and 
I absolutely love it--it's great for self-studying and
for a year-long high school class.

Online videos are also a great way to learn
Calculus, especially if you have hard time
learning from a big lecture.
